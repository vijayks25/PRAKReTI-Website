What we do

Carbon credit project development

Advising project developers in project registration, development, certification, and trading to provide the best possible offers in the global carbon markets.

Digital MRV

Digital and automated MRV to revolutionize carbon monitoring for the verifiers. Measuring, reporting & verification (MRV), is the cornerstone of legitimate carbon credits issuance. But the industry is still run on Excel sheets & PDF files. Our automation solutions will result in 10x time reduction in verification per officer.

Carbon trading

PrakReti tokenizes carbon credits, which are certificates that represent a project's positive impact on the planet. Our platform will offer unique features like carbon pools, where similar carbon credits are pooled together and connected to decentralized finance apps. Carbon pools create liquidity and improve the ease of transacting in the environmental markets.
